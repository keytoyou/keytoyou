Purpose

What it is

Our project is centralized on improving the moods of our users. By selecting an emotion on our question page, we generate a quote from our databases to assist in their needs. Whether feeling stressed or sad, we attempt to alleviate the worries for our users even if for just a second.

Why We Created This

This summer we participated in the Girls Who Code summer immersion program where we learned a variety of coding topics. From creating sidescroller games to programing robots to dance to music we had a blast. As for this website, we both have a big appreciation for quotes and wanted to incorporate that into our project. We have had so much fun creating something that encourages others to reach their fullest potential.

FAQ

How do I get a quote? : By navigating to the question page, one should choose a landscape representative of their current mood. From there they will be navigated to a quote tailored to your needs.

How do I return to the home page? : By clicking our logo, located on the upper left of any page, you can return to the home screen.

How does it work? : Each quote is categorized by mood in arrays which we created an algorithm to randomly select a quote from and display it on the webpage.
